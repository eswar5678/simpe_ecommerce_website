Our Story

More Than Just a Grocery Store

Since 1987, Haven's Corner has been serving our community with the freshest local products and personal service you won't find at big chains. What started as a small produce stand run by Eleanor and Robert Henderson has grown into a beloved neighborhood institution.

We believe in supporting local farmers and artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every jar of jam, loaf of bread, and wheel of cheese on our shelves comes with a story — and often, a handshake.

Our family is your family. We know your name, your favorite snacks, and even your dog's name. Come in for groceries, stay for conversation. That's what makes this place special.

"I've shopped here since I was a child. The sourdough still tastes just like my grandmother used to buy. This store feels like home."

Meet the Family

Today, our daughter Sarah runs the shop alongside her husband Mark, continuing the traditions while adding new favorites like our weekly farmer's market pickup and community cooking classes.
